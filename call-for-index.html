Submission Guidelines

We invite submissions of abstracts related to the theme of quantitative syntax. The submissions may cover topics including (but not limited to):

  • Statistical models of syntax
  • Corpus-based studies
  • Machine learning applications to syntactic theory
  • Comparative syntactic analysis

Important Dates

  • Abstract Submission Deadline: January 15, 2025
  • Notification of Acceptance: February 15, 2025

Submission Format

Abstracts should be a maximum of 500 words in PDF format. Please use our submission portal linked here.
