Welcome to QUASY 2025

QUASY 2025, the third workshop in the QUASY series, is dedicated to advancing quantitative approaches in syntactic research. This year, QUASY will be held in Ljubljana, Slovenia, from August 26-29, 2025, as part of SyntaxFest 2025.

QUASY brings together researchers interested in exploring syntactic theory through quantitative, statistical, and computational methods. We encourage contributions that address all areas of syntax using empirical data, including corpus studies, treebank analyses, and statistical modeling.
