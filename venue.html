Venue

The conference will take place at the Faculty of Law, University of Ljubljana (Poljanski nasip 2, 1000 Ljubljana). Located in the center of Ljubljana, the venue is just a short walk from the Ljubljanica River, the local market, and the iconic Dragon Bridge, which highlights the city's mythical connection to dragons.

Travel

Ljubljana is conveniently located between Italy, Central Europe, and the Balkans, making it easily accessible by road, rail, or air travel. Traveling within Ljubljana is straightforward, with well-connected public transport, pedestrian-friendly areas, and bike rentals. The city’s charming old town, vibrant local markets, and the beautiful Ljubljanica River are all easily accessible, making exploring the city a delightful experience.

More information about accomodation, transport and other practical details will be provided later.
