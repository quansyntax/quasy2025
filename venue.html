Venue

The conference will take place at the Faculty of Law, University of Ljubljana (Poljanski nasip 2, 1000 Ljubljana). Located in the center of Ljubljana, the venue is just a short walk from the Ljubljanica River, the local market, and the iconic Dragon Bridge, which highlights the city's mythical connection to dragons.

About Ljubljana

Ljubljana, the capital of Slovenia, is a charming city known for its beautiful architecture, vibrant culture, and green spaces. The city center is largely pedestrianized, making it pleasant to explore on foot. The iconic Ljubljana Castle overlooks the city, offering panoramic views.

Travel

Ljubljana is conveniently located between Italy, Central Europe, and the Balkans, making it easily accessible by road, rail, or air travel. Traveling within Ljubljana is straightforward, with well-connected public transport, pedestrian-friendly areas, and bike rentals. The city's charming old town, vibrant local markets, and the beautiful Ljubljanica River are all easily accessible, making exploring the city a delightful experience.

Getting to Ljubljana

Ljubljana is served by Ljubljana Jože Pučnik Airport (LJU), located approximately 25 kilometers from the city center. Several airlines operate flights to Ljubljana from major European cities.

More information about accommodation, transport and other practical details will be provided later.

Weather

Ljubljana in August typically has pleasant summer weather with average temperatures around 22-26°C (72-79°F). Evenings can be cooler, so a light jacket is recommended.
